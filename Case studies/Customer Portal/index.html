Utility Customer Portal

Project Duration: 2023-2024 | Role: Product Owner

Key Achievements

  • 40% increase in digital adoption
  • 25% reduction in call center volume
  • 35% improvement in user engagement metrics
  • $2M annual cost savings

Project Overview

Led the development and launch of a comprehensive customer portal for a major US utility company serving 3M+ customers. The project aimed to digitize customer interactions, improve self-service capabilities, and enhance overall customer experience.

The Challenge

Business Problems
  • High call center volume (100K+ calls/month)
  • Low digital adoption rate (25%)
  • Poor customer satisfaction scores
  • High operational costs
User Pain Points
  • Complex bill payment process
  • Limited self-service options
  • Poor mobile experience
  • Lack of real-time usage insights

Research & Discovery

Method Participants Key Insights
User Interviews 50+ customers Need for simplified navigation and mobile access
Call Center Analysis 6 months of data 80% of calls were for basic transactions
Competitive Analysis 5 competitors Gap in digital features and user experience

Solution Design

Key Features Prioritized:

Phase 1 (MVP)
  • One-click bill payment
  • Usage dashboard
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Account management
Phase 2 (Enhancement)
  • Predictive analytics
  • Smart notifications
  • Energy saving insights
  • Chat support integration

Development Approach

Implemented an agile development methodology with two-week sprints. Key aspects included:

  • Cross-functional team of 20+ members
  • Regular user testing and feedback loops
  • Continuous integration and deployment
  • A/B testing for key features

Technical Implementation

Technology Stack:

  • Frontend: React.js, Material UI
  • Backend: Node.js, MongoDB
  • Cloud: AWS (ECS, Lambda, S3)
  • Analytics: Adobe Analytics, PowerBI
  • Integration: REST APIs, GraphQL

Results & Impact

Quantitative Results
  • 40% increase in digital adoption
  • 25% reduction in call volume
  • 35% improvement in user engagement
  • 15% increase in customer satisfaction
Business Impact
  • $2M annual operational savings
  • Improved customer retention
  • Enhanced digital presence
  • Better data-driven insights

Key Learnings

  1. User-Centric Design: Regular user feedback was crucial for feature prioritization
  2. Data-Driven Decisions: Analytics implementation from day one provided valuable insights
  3. Agile Methodology: Two-week sprints allowed for quick iterations and improvements
  4. Stakeholder Management: Regular alignment with business and technical teams was essential

Future Roadmap

  • AI-powered customer service chatbot
  • Integration with smart home devices
  • Enhanced predictive analytics
  • Personalized energy saving recommendations
